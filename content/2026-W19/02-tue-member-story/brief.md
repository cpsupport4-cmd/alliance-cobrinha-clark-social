# TUE · Member Story

**Pillar:** Member Spotlights (2–3× per week)
**Drive source:** `Member Spotlights`
**Format preference:** Quote card / short clip / portrait photo
**Goal:** Relatability → conversion — converts better than ads

## Featured member

Each Tuesday spotlights one student. Rotate through:

- Beginners
- Parents (especially powerful for the THU kids/family pillar)
- Professionals
- Kids (with parental OK)

## Caption tone

First-person if possible, third-person otherwise. Short. Honest. No production polish.

- "Started with zero experience. Still here 3 months later."
- "Why I started Jiu-Jitsu — and why I stayed."
- "Three stripes. Three months. One reason: the people."

## Brief override

Name the member, their role/journey in 1–2 sentences, and any quote you want featured. Skill will lean into it instead of generating generic copy.
