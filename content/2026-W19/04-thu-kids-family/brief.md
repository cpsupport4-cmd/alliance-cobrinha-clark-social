# THU · Kids / Family **(CRITICAL)**

**Pillar:** Community Content (kids angle)
**Drive source:** `Kids & Family`
**Format preference:** Photo or event poster
**Goal:** Parent conversion — this is the most important slot of the week

## Why this slot matters

50% of our roster is kids 6–12. Their parents are the actual conversion target — they watch class from the sideline and decide whether to keep paying. Thursday's job is to remind them why.

## Caption tone

Reassuring, parent-facing. Lead with what kids gain, not what they do.

- "Confidence. Listening. A safe place to learn."
- "What 45 minutes on the mat builds in a kid you can't see in a school photo."
- "Parents — this is what they look like after class."

**Avoid:** anything that sounds like recruitment for adults. The reader is a parent.

## Brief override

If a specific kid's milestone, a stripe ceremony, or a parent quote should anchor the post, drop it here.
